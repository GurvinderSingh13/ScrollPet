FAQ Content (Use Exactly This)
1. What is ScrollPet?
ScrollPet is a community platform where pet lovers, breeders, and pet owners can connect with each other. It offers chat rooms based on different pets, breeds, and locations, making it easy to join discussions about pets you care about.
________________________________________
2. How do I join chat rooms?
You can join chat rooms based on your pet type (e.g., dog, cat) and even by breed (e.g., Pug, Siamese). You can also join rooms for your country, state, or local district, as well as a global chat room where users from all over the world can chat.
________________________________________
3. What is the News Room?
Each chat room (based on pet or breed) has its own News Room, where the ScrollPet team posts updates. These could include:
•	Pet-related news and updates
•	Announcements about the platform
•	Promotions or special offers on pet products or services
Only the ScrollPet team can post in the News Room; users can only read or view the posts.
________________________________________
4. What content can I share?
You can share text messages, images, videos, and voice notes in the chat rooms. However, everything shared must be related to pets and must follow the community guidelines.
________________________________________
5. Who moderates the chat rooms?
Both Company Moderators and Community Moderators manage the chat rooms:
•	Company Moderators can permanently ban users who break the rules.
•	Community Moderators can issue temporary bans and help manage discussions.
________________________________________
6. What happens if I break the rules?
Violating the rules may lead to a temporary ban from posting in chat rooms. You’ll still be able to read the chat but won’t be able to participate. Repeated violations could result in a permanent ban.
________________________________________
7. How can I report or block a user?
To report or block someone, click on their message or profile and choose from the available report options. This helps keep the community safe and respectful.
________________________________________
8. What pet categories are available?
ScrollPet has nine main pet categories: Dogs, Cats, Horses, Fish, Rabbits, Birds, Hamsters, Turtles, and Other Pets. Each category has sub-categories for different breeds.
________________________________________
9. Can I delete my account?
Currently, account deletion is not available, but this feature will be added in the future.
________________________________________
10. What languages are supported?
•	Global chat: Only English
•	National chats: National language (e.g., Hindi for India)
•	State/District chats: Both national and regional languages are allowed
English can be used in any chat room.
________________________________________
11. How do I become a Community Moderator?
Active, helpful, and respectful users may be invited by the ScrollPet team to become Community Moderators.
________________________________________
12. Can I switch between chat rooms?
Yes, you can switch between chat rooms any time. You can choose different rooms based on the pet category, breed, or location that interests you.
________________________________________
13. What should I do if I see inappropriate content?
You can report inappropriate content by clicking on the message and selecting the report option. This helps keep the community safe and enjoyable for everyone.
________________________________________
14. Can I suggest new pet categories?
Yes, if there’s a pet category or breed you’d like to see added, you can suggest it. The ScrollPet team will review your suggestion.
________________________________________
15. What should I do if I’m banned from a chat room?
If you’re banned, review the community guidelines and wait until the ban is lifted. You can contact support if you think the ban was unfair.
________________________________________
16. How do I update my profile?
You can update your profile information in the account settings section.
________________________________________
17. How can I provide feedback?
You can share your feedback or suggestions through the feedback section or by contacting customer support. Your feedback helps improve ScrollPet.
