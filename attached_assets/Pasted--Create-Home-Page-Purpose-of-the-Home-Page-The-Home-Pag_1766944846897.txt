Purpose of the Home Page

The Home Page is the main entry point of ScrollPet.
Its purpose is to introduce the platform, help users understand what ScrollPet is, and guide them to chat rooms or login with minimal friction.

Header (Top Navigation Bar)

The header is visible on all pages and stays consistent.

Header Menu Items

From left to right:

Home – Returns the user to the homepage

Chat Rooms – Takes users to pet-based chat rooms

About Us – Explains what ScrollPet is and its mission

FAQ – Answers common user questions

Contact Us – Allows users to reach the ScrollPet team

Authentication Button (Right Side)

Before login:

Show Login button

After login:

Show Logout button

(Optional later: show user profile icon)

This makes login status clear at all times.

Main Home Page Content
1. Hero Section (Top Section)

A friendly headline, for example:
“A Community for Pet Lovers, Built Around Trust and Care”

Short supporting text explaining ScrollPet in simple words

Primary call-to-action button:

“Join the Community” (leads to Login / Signup)

If already logged in → “Go to Chat Rooms”

2. What Is ScrollPet? (Intro Section)

A short explanation of the platform:

ScrollPet is a chat-first community platform for pet lovers

Users connect based on:

Pet type

Breed

Location

Focused on respectful discussion and trusted information

This section builds understanding and trust.

3. How It Works (Simple Steps)

Displayed as 3–4 simple steps:

Sign up or log in

Choose your pet category

Join local or global chat rooms

Learn and connect with other pet lovers

Very simple, visual, and easy to understand.

4. Community & Safety Section

Brief explanation that highlights trust:

Moderated chat rooms

Clear community rules

Reporting and blocking features

Admin-controlled news posts

Purpose: reassure new users that the platform is safe and serious.

5. Footer (Bottom Section)

The footer repeats important links:

Home

Chat Rooms

About Us

FAQ

Contact Us

Optional footer text:

“© ScrollPet – Built for pet lovers”
